The online store tSUNAMI sTORE was born in 2017 as an ambitious project by two young entrepreneurs who shared a great passion for technology and digital commerce. They realized that many stores didn’t offer a personalized shopping experience or innovative products at fair prices, so they decided to create a fresh and modern alternative. They started selling from home with a small catalog that included phone cases, wireless headphones, and portable gadgets. Thanks to word of mouth and a strong presence on social media, tSUNAMI sTORE quickly gained popularity. As demand grew, tSUNAMI sTORE expanded its catalog to include emerging brands and cutting-edge products such as smartwatches, tablets, drones, and smart home devices. From the beginning, they focused on fast logistics, quick shipping, and real-time customer support, which helped them build a loyal community of demanding but satisfied customers. The year 2020 marked a turning point with the international expansion of the business and a complete redesign of their website, optimized for mobile and aimed at delivering a seamless shopping experience. Today, tSUNAMI sTORE is much more than an online shop—it's a brand that inspires trust and closeness in the world of technology. Its community continues to grow thanks to the content shared on its blog and social media, where they explain trends, product launches, and tech comparisons. With a strong identity and a clear focus on innovation, tSUNAMI sTORE continues to stand out in a constantly evolving market.
At tSUNAMI sTORE, our mission is to bring technology closer to everyone by offering innovative, accessible, and high-quality products, along with a fast, secure, and personalized shopping experience. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and building a well-informed, tech-loving community. We also believe that technology should be a tool for empowerment. That’s why we don’t just sell devices—we educate, inspire, and support our customers so they can get the most out of every product. We want every purchase at tSUNAMI sTORE to be the beginning of a positive and transformative experience.
Our vision is to become the leading online store for tech products in the Spanish-speaking market, recognized for our reliability, constant innovation, and commitment to sustainability. At tSUNAMI sTORE, we dream of a future where technology is accessible, useful, and improves the life of every user. We envision a digital ecosystem where people don’t just shop, but also learn, share, and connect through technology. We want to lead that change, being a brand that evolves alongside its users, always tuned in to new trends and the real needs of the modern consumer.
